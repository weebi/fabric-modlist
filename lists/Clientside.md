# Clientside mod list

> **Note**
> Most mods require you to install [Fabric API](https://modrinth.com/mod/P7dR8mSH)!

## Audio

- [Charmonium](https://modrinth.com/mod/bpii4Xsa) - *Biome-specific sound effects, underground sound effects and much more.*

- [Dynamic Surroundings: Remastered](https://modrinth.com/mod/4TSI7dRt) - *Alters the player's visual and audible experience without changing game mechanics.*

- [More Music](https://modrinth.com/mod/dGBEUH8l) - *A music mod that makes in-game music play more or less often*

- [OstOverhaul](https://modrinth.com/mod/M11EPNE5) - *Adds more place-dependant music to the game.*

- [Presence Footsteps](https://modrinth.com/mod/rcTfTZr3) - *Adds more dynamic sounds for every block the player walks on.*

- [Resounding](https://modrinth.com/mod/UwbKhrqq) - *An audio mod that brings realistic audio physics into the game.*

- [Sound Physics Remastered](https://modrinth.com/mod/qyVF9oeo) - *Adds realistic sound attenuation, reverberation, and absorption through blocks.*

## Bug fixes

- [Debugify](https://modrinth.com/mod/QwxR6Gcd) - *Bugfixes for things on the Mojang bug tracker.*

## Graphics

- [3D Skin Layers](https://modrinth.com/mod/zV5r3pPn) - *Replaces the flat second layer of player skin with a 3d modeled version.*

- [Better Animations Collection](https://modrinth.com/mod/OoOVj3J3) - *Adds a bunch of animations and subtle effects.*

- [Better Biome Blend](https://modrinth.com/mod/Rs6c7WyL) - *Improves biome color transitions with larger biome blending radius.*

- [BetterBlockOutline](https://modrinth.com/mod/tBrBqOsV) - *Allows ample customisation of the block outline.*

- [Better Enchanted Books](https://modrinth.com/mod/yjpXhps7) - *Assigns custom colored straps to enchantment books.*

- [Blur](https://modrinth.com/mod/NK39zBp2) - *Blurs the backgrounds of GUIs in-game.*

- [Bobby](https://modrinth.com/mod/M08ruV16) - *Allows greater render distances by caching the chunks to the player's hard drive.*

- [Capes](https://modrinth.com/mod/89Wsn8GD) - *Renders capes from OptiFine, LabyMod and other cape mods.*

- [CIT Resewn](https://modrinth.com/mod/otVJckYQ) - *Implements Custom Item Textures (CIT) for resource packs.*

- [Clear Skies](https://modrinth.com/mod/xNK6Xfrv) - *Removes the banding at the horizon of vanilla Minecraft.*

- [Colormatic](https://modrinth.com/mod/V4IQxkZC) - *Allows resource packs to customize hard coded block and biome colors.*

- [Continuity](https://modrinth.com/mod/1IjD5062) - *Enables resource packs to create connected textures.*

- [Eating Animation](https://modrinth.com/mod/rUgZvGzi) - *Adds eating animation for vanilla items.*

- [Entity Texture Features](https://modrinth.com/mod/BVzZfTc1) - *Random, emissive and blinking textures for mobs and player skins.*

- [Falling Leaves](https://modrinth.com/mod/WhbRG4iK) - *Adds a neat particle effect to leaf blocks.*

- [First-Person Model](https://modrinth.com/mod/H5XMjpHi) - *Shows the third-person model in first-person.*

- [Hat List](https://modrinth.com/mod/nHLxbmEC) - *Shows the player skin's hat layer on the player list.*

- [Indium](https://modrinth.com/mod/Orvt0mRa) - *Add-on for Sodium to add support for the Fabric Rendering API.*

- [Iris](https://modrinth.com/mod/YL57xq9U) - *Shader mod compatible with OptiFine shaderpacks.*

- [Item Borders](https://modrinth.com/mod/b1fMg6sH) - *Adds colored borders to items to make your rare items stand out.*

- [Kappa](https://modrinth.com/mod/2wsNAbi3) - *Adds OptiFine capes correctly into the game.*

- [LambDynamicLights](https://modrinth.com/mod/yBW8D80W) - *Adds dynamic lighting to the game.*

- [LambdaBetterGrass](https://modrinth.com/mod/2Uev7LdA) - *Implements Better Grass feature from OptiFine but better.*

- [Not Enough Animations](https://modrinth.com/mod/MPCX6s5C) - *Adds many different third-person animations to Minecraft.*

- [Particle Blocker](https://modrinth.com/mod/aVja52oo) - *Allows blocking specific particles from rendering.*

- [Particle Rain](https://modrinth.com/mod/nrikgvxm) - *Replaces the default rain and snow with particle effects.*

- [Rainbowify](https://modrinth.com/mod/m9FFV06N) - *Adds a customisable rainbow background to Minecraft GUIs*

- [True Darkness](https://modrinth.com/mod/Ja7zPEaN) - *Makes the darkness pitch black.*

- [Wavey Capes](https://modrinth.com/mod/kYuIpRLv) - *Makes the capes look like capes and not like a slab.*

## Optimization

- [Bedrodium](https://modrinth.com/mod/5roWs6VO) - *Slightly optimises the bedrock layer.*

- [BetterBeds](https://modrinth.com/mod/kKwy3HU9) - *Changes the renderer for beds to use a more optimised json model.*

- [Concurrent Chunk Management Engine](https://modrinth.com/mod/VSNURh3q) - *Improves the performance of chunks in the world.*

- [Dynamic FPS](https://modrinth.com/mod/LQ3K71Q1) - *Reduces Minecraft's FPS when the window isn't focused on.*

- [Enhanced Block Entities](https://modrinth.com/mod/OVuFYfre) - *Optimizes block entities and adds customisation features.*

- [EntityCulling](https://modrinth.com/mod/NNAgCjsB) - *Skips rendering the blocks/entities that aren't visible to the player.*

- [Entity Collision FPS Fix](https://modrinth.com/mod/GiriLmaY) - *Skips entity collision checks on the client (these are handled on the server).*

- [Exordium](https://modrinth.com/mod/DynYZEae) - *Renders the GUI and screens at a lower framerate to speed up the world rendering.*

- [FerriteCore](https://modrinth.com/mod/uXXizFIs) - *Optimises memory usage on clients and servers.*

- [ForgetMeChunk](https://modrinth.com/mod/vRXn3MrA) - *Fixes large lag spikes when crossing chunk borders.*

- [Language Reload](https://modrinth.com/mod/uLbm7CG6) - *Makes language switching instant.*

- [LazyDFU](https://modrinth.com/mod/hvFnDODi) - *Speeds up the startup by making the DataFixerUpper "lazy" at boot.*

- [Lithium](https://modrinth.com/mod/gvQqBUqZ) - *Game logic optimisations for both servers and clients.*

- [Memory Leak Fix](https://modrinth.com/mod/NRjRiSSD) - *Fixes multiple memory leaks in Minecraft.*

- [Phosphor](https://modrinth.com/mod/hEOCdOgW) - *Vanilla's lighting engine optimisations.* **(Incompatible with Starlight)**

- [RandomPatches](https://modrinth.com/mod/JmtW1Cr5) - *Miscellaneous and configurable patches for the game.*

- [Sodium](https://modrinth.com/mod/AANobbMI) - *Rendering engine and client-side optimisations.*

  - [Reese's Sodium Options](https://modrinth.com/mod/Bh37bMuy) - *Replaces Sodium's default options screen with a better one.*

  - [Sodium Extra](https://modrinth.com/mod/PtjYWJkn) - *Adds extra settings to Sodium.*

- [Starlight](https://modrinth.com/mod/H8CaAYZC) - *Rewrites Minecraft's entire lighting engine to fix bugs and performance issues.* **(Incompatible with Phosphor)**

- [NoRefreshScroll](https://modrinth.com/mod/bWkJ7ejc) - *Stops server list from scrolling to the top when refreshing it.*

- [Preventer](https://modrinth.com/mod/RK0tTWG1) - *Prevents you from doing dumb things by accident.*

- [Right-Click Harvest](https://modrinth.com/mod/Cnejf5xM) - *Harvest and replant crops by right-clicking them.*

- [Screenshot to Clipboard](https://modrinth.com/mod/1K1JRrTg) - *A simple mod that copies the taken screenshot to the clipboard.*

- [Tooltip Tweaks](https://modrinth.com/mod/evJ7VC0w) - *Adds new tooltips and improves the existing ones while allowing to disable the ones you dislike.*
